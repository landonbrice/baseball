# Pitcher Lifting Research Dossier (Velocity + Health)

_Last updated: 2026-03-14_

## What this project is
A practical, evidence-informed guide to **in-season and off-season lifting for baseball pitchers** with two goals:
1. Increase/maintain velocity
2. Reduce injury risk (especially shoulder/elbow)

This dossier explicitly queried peer-reviewed literature (primarily PubMed/NCBI E-utilities) and integrated high-quality applied sources, including Driveline and Tread materials.

## How to use this
1. Read `01_Evidence_Summary.md` first.
2. Choose your season phase (in-season/off-season).
3. Pick a split from `04` and a template from `06`.
4. Adjust volume by mound workload and readiness, not ego.

## Important framing
- Most baseball lifting evidence is **small-sample and heterogeneous**.
- Strongest practical approach: combine best available evidence + individualized monitoring.
- Avoid all-or-nothing camps ("heavy always" vs "never lift in-season").
