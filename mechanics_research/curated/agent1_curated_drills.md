## A) HIP ROTATION — *back hip drives and clears INTO a posted, blocked lead leg*

**Fault:** good drop, **no rotate**. The back hip leaks and never clears; the drop→rotate
transition stalls; the lead leg doesn't post/block. Worst diagnostic: hip-explosion-sequence = 2
(he spins, he doesn't sequence).

**Fault:** front hip swings open early, lead leg lands open, shoulders fly open, trunk not
neutral → the separation window never opens. Worst diagnostics: glove-side-at-foot-strike = 1,
pelvis-shoulder differential = 2, lead-foot-rotation = 2.

## C) ARM PATH / SPIRAL — *arm laid back and WHIPPED by the trunk; spiral, not push*

layback; the arm isn't pulled by the trunk. Diagnostics: elbow-leads-forearm = 3,
release-extension = 3. **Heavy Bauer territory** per the user.
