## Arm Care Light
*Goal: Restore lost ROM, enhance recovery and healing through light contraction, high reps, blood flow*
*Always do soft tissue work first (as needed)*

- Arm car standing — 1x20 each
- Pot stir — 1x20-30 swings each direction
- Chair pose snow angel — 2x10 sec hold into 5 active reps
- High plank to yoga push up — 1x10
- World's greatest — 3 reps each leg
- Posterior capsule hip rotation — 10 sec hold to 10 rotations
- Scorpion — 1x8 each
- Prone T ER roll — 2x10
- Weighted PNF — 2x10 each
- Bridge reach — 2x16 touches (8 each)
- Modified sleeper contract relax — 2 rounds, 3 bouts, 10/5 contract/5 contract
- Elbow flexed pronation supination — 2x20
- Moving flexor iso
- Pronator press out — 2x10
- Nerve glide — 2x10
- Lat hang — 2x30 sec

## Plyocare Routines

### Full Plyo Heavy (50-80% effort)
- Level 1: 9/7oz, 8-10 each — Lay back (upper arm spiral feel)
- Level 2: 9/7oz, 8-10 each — High 10
- Level 3: 9/7oz, 3-5 each — Step stretch
- Level 4: Red/yellow band, 3-5 each — QB drop backs
- Level 5: 7/5oz, 3-5 each — In the hole
- Level 6: 7/5oz, 3-5 each — Flow wind up

### Half Plyo (40-70% effort)
- Level 1: 9/7oz, 8-10 each — Lay back
- Level 2: 9/7oz, 8-10 each — High 10
- Level 3: 9/7oz, 3-5 each — Step stretch
- Level 4: Red/yellow band, 3-5 each — QB drop backs

### Recovery Throws
- Ball holds (black ball) — 20 throws, 50-70%
- Sock throws — 7/5oz, 10 each, 50-70%
- Decel swings (black or 2.5kg cuffs) — 20 throws, 50-70%
- Pec load throw — 14/9oz, 8-10 each, 50-70%
- Figure 8 — 9/7oz, 8-10 each, 50-70%

### Pre-Bullpen Plyo (smooth/controlled)
- Level 1-6 with 14/9oz bands, 1 set / smooth effort
- Warmup toss to tolerance
- Bullpen
