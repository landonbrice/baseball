# Throwing Ramp-Up Program (6 Weeks)
# Source: Ramp up with Bullpen ramp up (1).xlsx
# Classification: Template for returning pitchers to full throwing

---

## Overview
Adapted 6-week progressive throwing program with pre-throw drills.
Pre-throw includes: uphill throws on wall, sock throws, high pec load at 30ft.

## Drill Progression by Distance
- **45ft (50% intent):** High/pec load 10 at 30, snap-snap rocker x5, self toss x5
- **60ft (60% intent):** Figure 8 rocker 25%, half kneel start 25%, in the hole 25%, step back 25%
- **75ft (70% intent):** QB drop back 50%, lateral bound 50%
- **90ft (75% intent):** Standard long toss
- **105ft (80% intent):** Extended distance
- **120ft (85% intent):** Max distance extension

## Key Principles
- Volume increases gradually (~30-40% week-over-week max)
- Compression/on-a-line throws introduced in Week 4
- Always warm up through lower distances before extending
- Work back in at 60ft after max distance on many days
