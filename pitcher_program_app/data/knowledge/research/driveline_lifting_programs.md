## Program Design Philosophy

- Designed for pitchers with <2 years training experience (adaptable for more advanced)
- Uses Bondarchuk's exercise classification pyramid (GPE → SPE → SDE → CE)
- Most gym exercises are GPE/SPE — general preparation, not baseball-specific movements
- College athletes average 17lbs heavier, 24% stronger, 21% more lower-body power than HS
- Priorities: lean body mass, strength, explosive strength
- RIR = Reps In Reserve (e.g., 2RIR = use weight you could barely do 2 more reps with)
- Goblet squat can be substituted with front squat for experienced lifters

### In-Season Considerations
- Weight room volume must decrease as competition volume increases
- Maintain high intensity (how hard) even while reducing frequency and volume (how often/much)
- Don't stop lifting in-season — you should still lift hard
- Soreness is OK, but manage frequency and volume carefully

### At-Home Modifications (when gym access limited)
- Longer eccentric tempos (slower on the way down)
- Pausing at bottom of rep
- Increasing reps
- Switching to unilateral variations (lunges)
- Prioritize finding gym access — light loads miss key benefits

## Off-Season Lifting (6 Weeks, 3 Days/Week)

### Day 1 — Lower Emphasis + Push
**Plyometric/Sprint Work:**
- Rudiment Hops (Linear): Moderate, 2x5yds ea
- Single Leg Bounds: Moderate, 1x10yds ea
- Lateral 5-10-5: Near Maximal, 2x1ea
- Half Kneeling Sprint Starts: Near Maximal, 2x10yds ea

**Strength (supersets):**
- A1: Goblet Squat — 2-3 RIR, 3x6 → 4x6 → 3x6
- A2: Inverted Row — BW, 3x10 → 4x10 → 3x8
- B1: Weighted Pushup — 2-3 RIR, 3x8 → 4x8 → 3x8
- B2: Landmine RDL — 2-3 RIR, 3x10 → 4x8 → 3x8
- C1: DB Reverse Lunges — 3 RIR, 3x12ea → 3x10ea → 2x10ea
- C2: Swimmers — BW, 3x8
- D1: Band Reverse Fly — Light, 3x15 → 3x20 → 2x20
- D2: Farmer's Carry — Near Max, 3x20yds → 3x15yds → 2x15yds

### Day 2 — Lateral Power + Upper
**Plyometric/Sprint Work:**
- Pushup Sprint Starts: Near Maximal, 2x15yds ea
- Medball Step Behind Scoop: 6-8lbs, 2x3ea
- Double Heiden to Sprint: Near Maximal, 2x10yds ea
- HK Medball Slam to Double Heiden: 6-8lbs, 2x2ea

**Strength (supersets):**
- A1: Landmine Lateral Lunge — 2-3 RIR, 3x5ea → 4x5ea → 3x5ea
- A2: Neutral Grip Pullups — BW, 3x4-6 → 3x6-8 → 2x6-8
- B1: Half Kneeling DB Overhead Press — 3 RIR, 3x10ea → 3x8ea → 2x8ea
- B2: Cable Pallof Press — 2 RIR, 3x8ea → 3x6ea → 2x6ea
- C1: High Box Step Ups — 3 RIR, 3x8ea → 3x10ea → 2x10ea
- C2: T-Pushups — BW, 3x4-6ea → 3x5-7ea → 2x5-7ea
- D1: Cable External Rotations — 3-4 RIR, 3x12ea
- D2: Shoulder Internal Rotation ISO — Near Max, 3x10sec ea

### Day 3 — Posterior Chain + Press
**Plyometric/Sprint Work:**
- RHS (lateral): Moderate, 2x15yds ea → 2x5yds ea
- Shuffle 5-10-5: Near Maximal, 2x1ea → 3x1ea → 2x1ea
- Shuffle to Sprint: Near Maximal, 2x20yds ea → 3x20yds ea
- 45 Degree Bounds: Near Maximal, 2x3ea → 2x4ea → 2x3ea

**Strength (supersets):**
- A1: Trap Bar Deadlift — 2 RIR, 3x5 → 4x5 → 3x5
- A2: Deadbugs — BW, 3x6ea
- B1: Half Kneeling Landmine Press — 2 RIR, 3x6ea
- B2: DB Bulgarian Split Squats — 2-3 RIR, 3x8ea
- C1: DB SL Glute Bridge — 2-3 RIR, 3x10ea → 3x8ea → 2x8ea
- C2: Half Kneeling High Cable Row — 2 RIR, 3x8ea → 3x6ea → 2x6ea
- D1: Prone T Holds — BW, 3x20s → 3x25s → 2x25s
- D2: Suitcase Carry — Near Max, 3x10yds ea → 2x10yds ea

## In-Season Lifting (6 Weeks, 3 Days/Week — Reduced Volume)

### Day 1 — Lower + Push (trimmed)
**Plyometrics:** Rudiment Hops, Lateral 5-10-5, HK Sprint Starts, Broad Jumps
**Strength:**
- A1: Goblet Squat — 2-3 RIR, 3x6 → 4x6 → 3x6

## Periodization Pattern (sets across weeks)
- Weeks 1-3: Base volume (3 sets typical)
- Weeks 4-5: Peak volume (+1 set on compounds)
- Week 6: Deload (drop to 2 sets on most exercises)

## Key Exercise Notes
- Pull:push ratio should be ~2:1
- Plyometric/sprint work always comes BEFORE lifting
- Supersets (A1/A2, B1/B2, etc.) alternate between muscle groups for efficiency
- Rest periods: 1-2 min between sets depending on exercise intensity
