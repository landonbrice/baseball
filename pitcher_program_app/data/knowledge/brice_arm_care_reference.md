# Brice Arm Care Protocol — Reference Material
# Source: Brice Thoughts.xlsx
# Classification: Reference for bot LLM to draw from contextually (not a template)
# Use case: When pitchers with medial elbow/UCL history or significant arm care needs query the bot

---

## Daily Focal Points
- Visit trainer: heat/stim/manual work when available
- Heat on the arm prior to exercises when possible
- Compression sleeve throughout the day
- Towel around arm at night to prevent full elbow bend during sleep

---

## Day A — Soft Tissue → Mobilize → Activate

### Soft Tissue (find tight/tender spot, sink in 20-30 sec, then move body part in opposing direction 5-6 times)
- Medial scap
- Rotator cuff
- Pec smash
- Pronator smash
- Flexor smash
- Triceps smash
- Biceps smash

### Mobilize
- Thoracic rockback: x20
- Cleland slump: 3x20 sec
- Banded levator scap: x5 deep breaths
- Banded lat: x5 deep breaths
- Ulnar tensioner: 1x10, 10 sec hold last rep
- Median nerve glide: 1x10, 10 sec hold last rep

### Activate
- Wall sit serratus scoop: 2x12
- Wall sit T roll: 2x10
- Wall sit 90 roll: 2x15
- Eccentric IR: 2x15
- ER on knee: 2x15 reps + 4 assisted reps
- Flexor pressout: 2x12 with 5 sec hold
- Tricep holds: 2x8, 5 sec hold
- Full pronation: 2x15 (full range)
- Full supination: 2x15 (full range)
- Ulnar deviation: 2x15 (full range)
- Finger pop: 2x20
- Wrist 3-way: 2x20 sec each
- Modified sleeper: 2x30 sec

## Day B — Mobility Flow

### Mobility Sequence
- Frogger in/out: x6-8, deep breath at end range
- Child's breathe: x5 deep breaths
- Posterior capsule rotation: 20 sec stretch, 10 rotations
- Pigeon step-through: 2x6 each
- OH adductor rock: x5 each
- Femoral nerve glide: x10 head nods each

### ELDOA Positions (breathing-based positional holds)
- ELDOA C5/6: 2x5 deep breaths
- ELDOA T6/7: 2x5 deep breaths
- ELDOA T7/T8: 2x5 deep breaths

### Loaded Stretches
- Loaded lat: 2x5 deep breaths
- Loaded pec: 3x5 deep breaths
- Bridge pulley: 4x15 sec

### Thoracic Work
- Thoracic rotation to side bend: x3 at 3-4 different levels
- Wall sit side bend: 2x10 each

## Soft Tissue Work Guide (from Landon Starters xlsx)

### Rules
Find tight/tender spot, sink in and hang out for 20-30 seconds, then move body part in opposing direction 5-6 times.

### Upper Body Areas
- **Recommended**: RTC, anterior delt, bicep, flexor smash, medial scap, lat foam roll
- **Additional**: first rib, triceps, thoracic extension, TLJ IR

### Hip Areas
- TFL, quad, adductor, hamstring, calf
